Through our online accountability sessions, you will have the chance to question your local and regional officers on their work over the past six months!

Regional Officers: Your officers, elected in to work for ALL the UHI Campuses- working regionally within the Highlands and Islands Local Officers: Your officers, elected to work in local campuses working soley in their local college

Regional Officer Accountability

Pop by this session if you'd like to ask your Regional Officers about their past year in post! After some short videos, Iain Morrison, UHI Dean of Students, will be quizzing the individuals with questions submitted by students, and will open the floor for more questions. This session is very popolar each year, as its YOUR chance to ask how the Officers have ensured YOUR voices are heard.

Local Officer Accountability

With time slots available for each academic partner, this is perfect opportunity for you to find out what your representatives have been doing to improve the standard of your student experience. Dont forget to watch the local videos and prepare any questions you'd love to ask- the floor is yours!
