About

Adam Bardsley Tech Consultancy recently opened its doors to support SME and charity customers with their technology needs. For the last 20 years Adam has been both a dedicated practitioner and a successful leader in the HE and Charity sector and is excited to be bringing this to his own business.

Whether you're a students' union looking to improve your online offering, a local business looking to attract clients, or have an application idea the world simply has to see, we can help you!
